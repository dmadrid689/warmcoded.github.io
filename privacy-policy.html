Overview

Centwise is a unit price calculator app for Android. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data the app handles and how it is used.

Data We Collect

We do not collect, transmit, or share any personal information.

All data you enter in Centwise — product names, prices, quantities, and comparisons — is stored locally on your device only using Android's built-in local storage (Room database). This data never leaves your device and is never accessible to Warmcoded or any third party.

In-App Purchases

Centwise offers an optional one-time Pro upgrade processed through Google Play Billing. Warmcoded does not handle, process, or store any payment or billing information. All payment data is managed exclusively by Google and is subject to Google's Privacy Policy.

Third-Party Services

Centwise does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, crash reporting, or tracking SDKs. No data is sent to external servers at any time.

Data Export (Pro Feature)

The Pro tier allows you to export your comparison data as JSON or CSV files. These exports are initiated solely by you and saved directly to your device. Warmcoded does not receive, access, or transmit any exported files.

Children's Privacy

Centwise does not knowingly collect data from anyone, including children under the age of 13. No personal information is collected by the app at any time.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes in a future update, the revised policy will be posted at this URL with an updated effective date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy.
